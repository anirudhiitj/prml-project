## 🔧 WHAT'S UNDER THE HOOD

### Your DPRNN Model
```
2-speaker Input
    ↓
[Learned Encoder: 64 filters]
    ↓
[Bottleneck: 64->64 channels]
    ↓
[6 DPRNN Blocks]:
  - Intra-chunk BiLSTM (learns local acoustic patterns)
  - Inter-chunk BiLSTM (maintains speaker identity)
    ↓
[Soft Masking: 5 speaker masks]
    ↓
[Learned Decoder]
    ↓
5 Separated Waveforms
```

### Curriculum Learning (Why it works!)
```
Start with 2 speakers (easy)
    ↓ [Load checkpoint, expand to 3 speakers]
3 speakers (harder, but initialized well)
    ↓ [Load checkpoint, expand to 4 speakers]
4 speakers (even harder, but still initialized)
    ↓ [Load checkpoint, expand to 5 speakers]
5 speakers (hardest, but starting from strong base)
    ↓
RESULT: Much better 5-speaker separation than starting from scratch!
```

### Real-Time Progress Tracking
All metrics saved to JSON (`training_status_phase_*_*spk.json`):
- Current epoch progress
- Training/validation loss  
- SI-SNR metrics
- Learning rate
- Gradient norms
- Estimated time remaining (ETA)
- Best validation checkpoint info
